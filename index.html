Compared with traditional fonticon,fusi reveal their unique emotion and warmth from every icon within it.
they originate from well-known movie or music, or from conveniently graffiti.they become as abstract as possible and inherit spirital strength in the productions.
Fusi are not recommended to be used in simple icons or indicators, but in modules or individualised segement in child sites.
How to use

  1. Copy the entire fusifont directory into your project.

  2. Reference the location to your fusifonticon.css.
    <link rel="stylesheet" href="/css/fusifont/fusifonticon.css" >

  3. use fusi in your html.such as <i class="fusi fusi-shawshank"></i>
